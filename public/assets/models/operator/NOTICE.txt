operator.glb — realistic CS-style tactical operator, the runtime player-character
avatar. Rigged (mixamorig skeleton) with in-place skeletal clips: Idle, Walk, Run,
Jump.

Source: Adobe Mixamo — character "Ch15" + the "Basic Shooter Pack" animation set
(free to use). Exported from Mixamo as FBX, converted to a single glTF binary
(three.js FBXLoader -> GLTFExporter): non-metal PBR materials, material groups
coalesced to 2 primitives, hip (root-motion) translation stripped so clips play
in place, mesh welded + simplified (~23k tris) and pruned (~1.5 MB). The avatar
does not cast shadows and its animation is culled off-screen (perf).
